There is a kitchen where you can eat. You sit together around a table, and make a plan.

George asks, "What can we do?"

He counts on his fingers:

"We cannot hurt the prisoners, but we can make them afraid of us.

"We must feed them, but we can make the food taste bad.

"We can separate them.

"We can tell them not to speak.

"We can keep them awake for many hours.

"We can laugh at them.

"We can make them get bored.

"We can be very kind to one of them, to make the others feel we treat them unfairly.

Emerson smiles: "We must discover their dark secret."
